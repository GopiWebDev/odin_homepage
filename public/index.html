About Me

I'm Kakashi Hatake a shinobi of Konohagakure's Hatake clan. Famed as Kakashi of the Sharingan and the Copy Ninja, one of Konoha's most talented ninja, regularly looked to for advice and leadership despite his personal dislike of responsibility. To his students on Team 7, emphasizing the importance of teamwork. I received this lesson, along with the Sharingan, from my childhood friend, Obito Uchiha. After the Fourth Shinobi World War, I becomes Konoha's Sixth Hokage.
